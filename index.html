Boreas — Weather Derivatives

An interactive pricing app for temperature-based weather derivatives (HDD/CDD calls). Combines Ornstein–Uhlenbeck processes, machine learning (Ridge, Random Forest, XGBoost) and Monte-Carlo simulation — with climate profiling, prediction fan charts, Monte-Carlo pricing and sensitivity analysis (Greeks).

  • Python
  • Streamlit
  • Ornstein–Uhlenbeck
  • Monte-Carlo
  • ML
